# How to Cancel Your Order - User Guide

## Quick Overview

You can cancel your order from the **Order History** or **Order Details** page. The refund amount depends on whether the kitchen has started preparing your order.

## How to Cancel

### Step 1: Find Your Order

**Option A: Order History Page**
1. Click on **"Orders"** in the navigation menu
2. Find the order you want to cancel
3. Look for the **"Cancel Order"** button

**Option B: Order Details Page**
1. Open your order confirmation email or notification
2. Click the link to view order details
3. Look for the **"Cancel Order"** button

### Step 2: Check Order Status

Before cancelling, check your order status:

- **"Not Yet Started Preparing"** (Green badge)
  - ‚úÖ Full refund available
  - Kitchen hasn't started cooking

- **"Preparation Started"** (Blue badge)
  - ‚ö†Ô∏è Partial refund only (50%)

  - You'll see a note: "50% refund if cancelled now"

- **"Completed"** (Gray badge)

  - Order already picked up

**Note**: If you don't see this button, your order cannot be cancelled (it's either ready or completed).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I cancel my order anytime?
**A**: No, you can only cancel up to 2 hours before your pickup time. This gives the kitchen time to adjust.

### Q: Why do I only get 50% refund if preparation started?
**A**: The kitchen has already used ingredients and staff time to prepare your food. The 50% retention covers these costs and helps reduce food waste.

### Q: How long does the refund take?
**A**: Refunds are typically processed within 3-5 business days to your original payment method.

### Q: Can I cancel a "Ready for Pickup" order?
**A**: No, once your order is ready, it cannot be cancelled as the food is already prepared.

### Q: What if I miss my pickup time?
**A**: Please contact the canteen staff. Late pickups may be handled differently than cancellations.

### Q: Can I cancel and reorder immediately?
**A**: Yes, but you'll need to wait for the refund to process. Consider modifying your order instead if possible.

### Q: What happens to the food if I cancel?
**A**: 
- Not started: No food waste
- Preparation started: We try to donate or repurpose when possible
- This is why we have the partial refund policy

### Q: Can I get a full refund even if preparation started?
**A**: No, the 50% refund policy is firm to cover costs and reduce waste. Please cancel early if you know you won't need the order.

### Q: How do I know if my order has started preparing?
**A**: Check your order status:
- "Not Yet Started Preparing" = Not started
- "Preparation Started" = Kitchen is cooking

### Q: What if there's an error with my order?
**A**: Contact canteen support immediately. Errors may be handled differently than regular cancellations.
